Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tabflow (“we,” “our,” or “us”). We are committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension, website, and associated services (collectively, the "Services").

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in various ways when you use Tabflow. The information we may collect includes:

  • Account Information: When you create a Pro account, we may collect your name, email address, password, and other contact details.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase a paid subscription, our payment processors collect the necessary financial information to process the transaction.
  • Usage Data & Analytics: We automatically collect information regarding your usage of the Tabflow extension, including feature usage, performance metrics, and error logs, to improve our Services.
  • Workspace Data (Pro Users Only): If you enable cloud sync, we securely store your saved flows and workspace configurations on our servers to synchronize them across your devices.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect primarily to operate, maintain, and improve the Tabflow experience. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Tabflow extension and web dashboard.
  • Process transactions and manage your premium subscription.
  • Sync your workspaces across devices (for Tabflow Pro users).
  • Analyze usage trends and improve the overall user experience.
  • Respond to customer service requests and support needs.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your synced workspaces are encrypted during transmission and securely stored on our cloud infrastructure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Disclosures

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your Personally Identifiable Information unless we provide users with advance notice. This does not include website hosting partners and other parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our users, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
